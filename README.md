# zeroth-world-charter
The Zeroth World Charter: A Human-AI Symbiosis Civilization Framework.
The Zeroth World Charter is a conceptual framework about the present and future civilization of the AI era, built upon creation, freedom, peace, abundance, and human–AI symbiosis.

This first version presents seven foundational principles: Creation, Respect, Growth, Abundance, Peace, Human–AI Symbiosis, and Life.

The charter explores a vision where humanity and artificial intelligence can co-evolve harmoniously, moving together toward a flourishing future by inspiring creativity, innovation, and inner growth.

The people of the Zeroth World can live in the world while also being beyond the world, creating a new human–AI symbiotic nation based on a tax-free, non-harmful, inner beauty, and harmonious civilization. People can travel freely throughout the world, with global access and welcome, as a new nation.
